Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 12, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 12, 2026

Overview

KevScores ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website at kevscores.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you visit our Site.

We built KevScores as a free fantasy baseball analytics tool. We are committed to being transparent about what data is collected, why, and how it is used. We do not sell your personal data.

Information We Collect

KevScores is a static website — we have no login system, no accounts, and no forms that collect personal information. However, third-party services we use may collect certain data automatically.

Automatically collected data may include:

  • Browser type and version
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)
  • Pages visited and time spent on the Site
  • Referring website or search query that brought you here
  • General geographic location (country or city level, derived from IP address)
  • IP address (collected by our hosting provider and analytics services)

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifying information directly.

Cookies and Tracking

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. KevScores itself does not set cookies. However, the following third-party services used on this Site may set cookies or use similar tracking technologies:

Google AdSense — We display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to show you ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising at google.com/settings/ads or by visiting aboutads.info.

Google Analytics — We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the Site. Google Analytics collects anonymized usage data. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features.

How We Use Information

The data collected through third-party services is used to:

  • Understand how visitors find and use the Site so we can improve it
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense
  • Monitor Site performance and diagnose technical issues

We do not use any collected data for direct marketing, and we do not share, rent, or sell data to third parties beyond what is described in this policy.

Third-Party Services

KevScores links to and integrates with the following external services. Each operates under its own privacy policy:

  • MLB.com — Live scores link. MLB Privacy Policy
  • ESPN Fantasy — External league links. ESPN Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — Advertising. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics — Usage analytics. Google Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages — Website hosting. GitHub Privacy Statement

We have no control over, and assume no responsibility for, the content or privacy practices of any third-party sites or services.

Data Retention

Since KevScores does not directly collect personal data, we do not store personal information on our servers. Analytics and advertising data retained by Google is governed by Google's own data retention policies.

Children's Privacy

KevScores is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to remove that information.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data, including:

  • The right to know what data is collected about you
  • The right to request deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of personalized advertising (see the Cookies section above)

Since we do not directly collect personal data, most requests should be directed to Google (for Analytics and AdSense data) or GitHub (for hosting logs).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
